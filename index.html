Concern: Increased School Timing at DPSG Meerut Road Ghaziabad

⚠️ Urgent: Students Facing Health Issues Due to Increased School Timings

Many students are suffering from exhaustion, stress, and health problems due to the increased school timings. Parents are seriously concerned about the physical and mental well-being of their children.

All parents are requested to take immediate action. Please submit complaints on the government portals listed below so that authorities take notice and protect our children.
